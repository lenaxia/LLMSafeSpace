## Key Decisions

1. **Three handlers stay separate.** `StreamEvents`, `StreamUserEvents`, and the reload handlers have distinct auth, route groups, and subscriber lifecycles. Consolidating the broker does not mean consolidating the handlers.

2. **`onDrop` wired for both subscribe paths.** The review correctly identified that `SubscribeUser` was ALSO missing `onDrop` (pre-existing bug, not introduced by this PR). Fixed both paths to ensure `brokerDroppedEvents` is populated for all subscriber types.

3. **Subscribe before headers in `StreamEvents`.** The old code wrote `200 OK` + SSE headers before subscribing (the old broker couldn't fail). Now that subscribe can fail, it must happen before headers are committed — mirroring `StreamUserEvents`.
