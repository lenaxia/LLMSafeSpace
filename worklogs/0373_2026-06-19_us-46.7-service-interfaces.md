# Worklog: US-46.7 Define Service-Shaped Interfaces

**Date:** 2026-06-19
**Status:** Complete

Defined 3 consumer-shaped interfaces (ISP extraction) for services with ≥2 consumers:

1. **MeteringRecorder** (2 methods: Record, RecordLifecycleEvent) — 3-4 record-only consumers (middleware, proxy events, app.go). Compile-time check added in metering package: `var _ interfaces.MeteringRecorder = (*Service)(nil)`.

2. **SettingsReader** (4 methods: GetBool, GetInt, GetString, GetStrings) — 6 read-only consumers (router, workspace_service, auth, rate_limit, max_active). Compile-time check can't live in pkg/settings (internal barrier) but is verified implicitly by consumer type assignments.

3. **WorkspaceMetadataStore** (2 methods: GetWorkspace, UpdateWorkspace) — 4 consumers of GetWorkspace (workspace_service, secrets_adapters, models_handler, database layer). Narrowed from the spec's original 4-method design to 2 methods that have the most overlap.

Audit found spec counts were partially inaccurate (spec said "8+ handlers" for database.Service; actually 3 in handlers/). All 3 interfaces verified justified by ≥2 real consumers.
