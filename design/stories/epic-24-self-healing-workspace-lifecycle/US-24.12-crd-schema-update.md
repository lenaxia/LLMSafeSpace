# US-24.12: CRD Schema Update

**Domain:** All
**Depends On:** US-24.4, US-24.5, US-24.13
**Priority:** High (ships with or after core stories)

## Summary

Update the Workspace CRD with new status fields, remove deprecated fields, and regenerate deepcopy.
